# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Coffee lovers, creators, students, and conversation-seekers in Bharuch, Gujarat. They arrive looking for a premium literary cafe experience — a space that combines exceptional coffee with the warmth of a neighbourhood living room.

## Product Purpose

Eighteen Stories is Bharuch's premium literary cafe on Tavra Road. A warm, dimly lit room where every cup is crafted by hand, every guest is welcomed like family, and stories are exchanged as freely as the espresso. Listed on Zomato with a ₹400-600 per person positioning.

## Brand Personality

Warm, grounded, literary, nocturnal, aspirational.

## Anti-references

- Generic AI-generated cafe websites with purple gradients and stock imagery
- Inter-only font stacks
- Bright, sterile, fast-casual chain aesthetics
- Template-driven hero-metric layouts
- Rounded, bubbly, soft-startup visual language

## Design Principles

1. **Dark as the default** — the palette lives in the brown-warm end of the spectrum, never pure black. The background is a room, not a void.
2. **Craft in every detail** — from the custom cubic-bezier transitions to the nested button icons, nothing ships generic.
3. **Typography as atmosphere** — Space Grotesk for headlines (bold, geometric, confident), Inter for body (readable, warm, unobtrusive).
4. **Motion with weight** — stagger reveals, fade-ups, and scroll-triggered animations that feel physical, not bouncy or decorative.
5. **The amber thread** — #E8A045 runs through every page as the connective accent: CTAs, underlines, glows, and highlights.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- Body text maintains 4.5:1 minimum contrast against dark backgrounds
- Reduced motion respected across all animations
- All interactive elements have visible focus states
- Content is never gated behind animation completion
